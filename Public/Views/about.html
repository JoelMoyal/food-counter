About us


We at 'Your Local Fruit Store' pay special attention to sustainability and a positive working environment for our farmers and our distributors. Our fruits are brought up with special attention, care and of course our secret ingredient: Love.

Since it is difficult to offer regional varieties all year round, especially in the area of fruit and vegetables, we have expanded our range to include organic fruit and vegetables and other organic foods. Bananas and tomatoes are also eaten with pleasure and only cabbage in winter is also not everyone's cup of tea. We always try to act so sustainably that we also rely in logistics on partners who balance your environmental balance again.
